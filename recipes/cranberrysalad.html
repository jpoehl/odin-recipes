Cranberry Salad

Description

This recipe describes how to make a delicious cranberry salad, in about an hour.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cranberries
  • 1 large orange
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup finely chopped walnuts
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • 1 cup crushed pineapple, drained
  • 1 (3 ounce) package raspberry flavored Jell-O© mix
  • 2 cups hot water

Steps

  1. Combine the gelatin with hot water (don't let stand).
  2. Grind cranberries and orange (including rind) mix with sugar.
  3. Stir in nuts, celery and pineapple.
  4. Mix with prepared gelatin and chill.
